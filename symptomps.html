Common Disease Symptoms

1. Influenza (Flu)

Symptoms:

  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Body aches
  • Sore throat
  • Fatigue

2. COVID-19

Symptoms:

  • Fever or chills
  • Dry cough
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue

3. Diabetes

Symptoms:

  • Increased thirst
  • Frequent urination
  • Extreme hunger
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Blurred vision

4. Malaria

Symptoms:

  • High fever
  • Chills and sweating
  • Headache
  • Muscle pain
  • Nausea and vomiting
