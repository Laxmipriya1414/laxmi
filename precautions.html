Precautions for Common Diseases

1. Flu Precautions

  • Get a flu vaccine every year
  • Wash hands frequently
  • Avoid close contact with sick people
  • Cover your mouth while sneezing

2. Cough Precautions

  • Stay hydrated and drink warm fluids
  • Avoid exposure to smoke and pollutants
  • Use a mask to prevent spreading germs
  • Take rest and use cough syrup if needed

3. Asthma Precautions

  • Avoid allergens like dust, pollen, and smoke
  • Use prescribed inhalers regularly
  • Exercise carefully and avoid overexertion
  • Monitor air quality before going outside

4. Cancer Prevention

  • Avoid smoking and alcohol
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables
  • Exercise regularly and maintain a healthy weight
  • Get regular medical checkups

5. Heart Attack Prevention

  • Maintain a healthy diet and avoid junk food
  • Exercise regularly to keep your heart strong
  • Control blood pressure and cholesterol levels
  • Reduce stress and avoid smoking

6. COVID-19 Precautions

  • Wear a mask in public places
  • Maintain social distancing
  • Wash hands frequently with soap
  • Get vaccinated and stay updated with booster shots
